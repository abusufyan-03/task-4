Maulana Azad

Maulana Abul Kalam Azad - An Indian scholar, freedom fighter, and the first Minister of Education in independent India.

Timeline of Maulana Azad's Life

  • 1888:Maulana Azad is born in Mecca, Saudi Arabia.
  • 1912:Azad becomes a prominent leader in the Indian National Congress.
  • 1947:India gains independence from British rule.
  • 1947-1958: Maulana Azad serves as the first Minister of Education in India.
  • 1953: Azad is posthumously awarded the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award.
